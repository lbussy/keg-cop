Calibrate Flowmeters

The flowmeter for this tap is now locked into calibration mode. Pull a measured amount of beverage through this tap and record the volume below. The "Pulses" and the PPU fields will update every 10 seconds. When you are finished, select "Update" and the PPU will be updated. You may select "Reset" if you wish to start over. In any case, accumulated pulses will be debited from the keg according to the currently configured PPU value.

The flowmeter for this tap is now locked into calibration mode. Enter the beverage's Specific Gravity if known. If not, a good value for beer would be 1.015. Tare your scale with a container and pull a reasonable amount of beverage through this tap. Weigh it and record the net weight below. The "Pulses" and the PPU fields will update every 10 seconds. When you are finished, select "Update" and the PPU will be updated. You may select "Reset" if you wish to start over. In any case, accumulated pulses will be debited from the keg according to the currently configured PPU value.

You can perform a simple reset on your Keg Cop by pressing the button below. This has the same effect as pressing the reset button on the controller, and will not harm your settings.

Reset WiFi

If you plan on relocating your Keg Cop to a new network, you should reset the WiFi connection settings.

If you are sure you want to completely reset your Keg Cop's WiFi configuration, click the red "Reset WiFi" button below. You will have to re-configure via the Access Point to join this device back to your local WiFi network.

Reset WiFi
