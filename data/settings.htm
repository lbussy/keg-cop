Reset Controller

You can perform a simple reset on your Keg Cop by pressing the button below. This has the same effect as pressing the reset button on the controller, and will not harm your settings.

Reset Controller

Update Application

Your Keg Cop can be updated over the internet to the latest version of the Keg Cop firmware by clicking the "Update Firmware" button below.

Update Firmware

Reset WiFi

If you plan on relocating your Keg Cop to a new network, you should reset the WiFi connection settings by clicking "Reset WiFi". You will need to perform the the original network setup again by connecting to this device as an access point.

Reset WiFi
